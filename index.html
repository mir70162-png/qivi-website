Leadership

Aijaz Mir Noohani

Founder & CEO, QIVI

Aijaz Mir Noohani is the founder of QIVI and is based in Badin, Sindh, Pakistan. With a background in AD Arts and over 5 years of experience as a professional English and Chinese translator, Aijaz developed a deep understanding of communication gaps in the digital world.


As an independent app developer, he saw how people across the globe spend hours on repetitive digital tasks - writing, replying, remembering, and organizing. This inspired the idea of QIVI in 2024: an AI that doesn’t just answer questions, but actually does the work for you.


"My vision for QIVI is simple: save the world’s time. Make work faster. Make solutions easier. I started QIVI to build an AI that remembers your work, writes for you, speaks for you, and handles your emails, WhatsApp, and social media - so you can focus on what truly matters."


Aijaz is currently leading QIVI’s development and testing, with a focus on building vertical AI that serves professionals, creators, and businesses across Pakistan and beyond.
